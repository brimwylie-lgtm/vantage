At Vantage Resourcing, we specialize in helping high-caliber, designated, or studying CPA's manage their accounting careers and land exciting roles that expand their skill set and experience.

Are you a CPA interested in growing your career?

What are you looking for? Want to make a move from industry accounting into an exciting role within public practice? Or, maybe you have come through the ranks and want to get to the next level?

Vantage's clients range from either larger, complex entities, through to, mid size, or, smaller high growth entities within both the Public and Private sectors. As industry leaders, they are looking for well-qualified CPA's with training within either a top 20 Accounting firm, or, experience gained through industry.

Strong technical ability, high emotional intelligence and first class interpersonal communication skills are also the hallmark of a Vantage applicant.

The Vantage team will assess your qualifications, interests, and personal values and then tap into Vantage's network to source opportunities that are a good fit for you. When we are confident we can place you, we will guide you through each step of the search process.

View jobs, browse through our candidate resources, or contact Vantage to discuss your accounting career and whether they can place or coach you.
