Vantage Resourcing's principal service is placing highly skilled accountants. When you are facing a shortage of highly qualified accountants, it can be easier and more cost effective than you might think to work with a quality centric, boutique search firm.

Why should you work with Vantage to augment your recruitment efforts?

Sheila and Nicole have a combined 35+ years' experience as senior accounting recruiters for the world's top firms in the United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. Vantage was founded in 2006 and has built a stellar reputation with a team that consistently supports select clients with their accounting staffing needs.

Because Vantage is a specialized, boutique firm, your search will have the attention, flexibility, and professionalism it deserves. Vantage invests the time to understand your unique needs and facilitate excellent results.

So, just how good are these Vantage CPA's?

Typically Vantage places designated CPA's, or, individuals working towards their qualification. They work with highly qualified Accountants who have gained their experience directly through a CPA firm, Industry, or, Commerce.

Candidates possess a high level of education, training, and experience and are "hallmarked" by their professionalism, interpersonal, and communication skills.
